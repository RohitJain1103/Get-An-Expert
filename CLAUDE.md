## Writing rule: no AI slop

Before shipping ANY written deliverable (website/UI copy, emails, messaging, social posts, docs, and the prose inside plans and artifacts), run the `no-ai-slop` skill and check the result against its `eval.md`. No em dashes. No stock AI phrasing (delve, leverage, empower, elevate, supercharge, "it's not X, it's Y", etc.). Preserve the writer's real voice: minimum effective edit, never flatten distinctive lines into generic polish.

Skill source: repo-local `.claude/skills/no-ai-slop/` if present, otherwise the global `~/.claude/skills/no-ai-slop/`. Both mirror github.com/petergyang/no-ai-slop.
